Registration

New students may enrol at any time during the year for most programs, and tuition fee may be allowed on a pro-rated basis. A completed and signed registration form along with registration fee must be received prior to student attending any lessons or classes.

 











About

Etude school of music is a registered firm in Chennai (India) that teaches both classical and contemporary forms of western music. Well qualified and experienced faculty in various departments impart sound knowledge and skills right from the rudiments of music and guide the students all the way up to achieve excellence in performing arts.
